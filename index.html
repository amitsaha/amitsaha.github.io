About

I am a programmer who likes to teach via his writings. I feel strongly in favor of the possibilities Open Source has opened up and try to keep all my codes/articles accessible to everyone with an appropriate license. Perhaps I should also say that I am not anti-Microsoft. The way they brought computers to the reach of the ordinary people was revolutionary. However, with the world changing around them, they should also change.

Why I believe in Open Source?

  • Open Source fosters sharing, and everything I learn and teach is meant to be shared with everyone.
  • Open Source allows you to follow your curiosity.
